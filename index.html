About Us

John McManus

Licensed Fiduciary, No. 1431

I was born in Oregon in 1997, raised in California's Central Coast for more than a decade along with my sister. My parents are involved in wealth, my dad in finance and my mother in accounting work. I attended Cuesta for 4 years, then graduated from Cal Poly in 2021 with a bachelor's degree in business administration, finance. From then on, I joined my father in his financial practice, earning the series 65 and the trust of his clients. I'm also a member of Cayucos Lions Club, serving as their Treasurer from 2021 to present day.
