About Secura Aide

We offer professional fiduciary services for those living in the Central Coast, be it for trusts, estates, conservatorships, or POA agreements. Our focus is on the estate, which includes your house, car, stocks, bonds, and savings.

Our Mission

Secura Aide's mission is to give its clients long term happiness.

Strategy

Its strategy is to service your financial, welfare, and health needs.

Vision

Our Vision involves three phases:

  1. We meet with you to understand your goals, needs, and desires, as well as the resources available.
  2. Develop a written plan which aims to accomplish those goals, needs, and desires with all available resources.
  3. We continue to meet with you to discuss, change, and maintain the plan as it relates to your goals, needs, and desires.

Our Services

Trustee Services

This includes trustee, successor trustee, and executor services.

Conservator Services

Focusing on either the person or the estate, this service is heavily tailored to the individual and their needs.

Agent Services

Includes acting as an agent in a Power of Attorney agreement (for health or finances) or as an administrator to your estate.
